Authoring ASCII, then crossing the bridge

A language model is asked to draw each subject as ASCII art from its name alone — the one thing in this whole research line that is authored, not just perceived. We then send that glyph-art through an image-to-image diffusion bridge and measure, in gemini-embedding-2 space, how far each stage travels toward a real photograph of the subject. Drag the slider.
