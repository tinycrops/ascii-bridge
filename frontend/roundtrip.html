The ASCII round trip

Human glyph-art is the one thing in this research line that is authored, not perceived. Here we push it through language and back into glyphs: a model reads a human ASCII drawing and writes a description of what it sees — without being told the subject — and then every model redraws brand-new ASCII from that description alone. The original drawing is never shown to the redrawers.
